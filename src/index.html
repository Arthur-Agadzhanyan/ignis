Portfolio of technologies

Baseload Power/Heat Generation from sedimentary basins

01

Re purposing old/dry/non economic wells with high temperature reservoir fluids to geothermal power/heat production

02

Drill new wells in brownfields with over 98% watercut to produce warm/hot temperature reservoir fluids for geothermal power/heat production

03

Co produce oil or gas with warm/hot reservoir fluids for clean and sustainable supply of power for O&G operations

Energy Downhole Storage

01

Store energy in old/dry/non economic wellbores in a form of a gravity battery storage with high power capacity

02

Store energy in old/dry/non economic wellbores in a form of a built up hydraulic pressure battery with high power capacity
